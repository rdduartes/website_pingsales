PingSales Lab · AI research

Where tomorrow's AI takes shape.

We focus on artificial intelligence research. The Lab is where we experiment without a safety net: we test models, build prototypes and turn hypotheses into systems our clients use months later.

Research lines

LAB / 01

Autonomous agents

AI systems that understand a goal, plan and execute - they serve, qualify, negotiate and report without human intervention. This is the line that powers the PingSales.ai brand.

LAB / 02

Applied language models

Taking large language models from generic to specific: tuned to each business's vocabulary, processes and knowledge, in Portuguese and in a European context.

LAB / 03

Intelligent process automation

Flows that do more than execute rules - they observe operations, detect patterns and propose the next step. Automation that improves with each cycle.

LAB / 04

Predictive data analytics

Models that anticipate demand, risk and customer behavior, turning a company's historical data into tomorrow's competitive advantage.

The Lab method

PHASE 01

Hypothesis

Every research project starts with a concrete business question: "what if an agent could do this alone?" Without a real question, there is no project.

PHASE 02

Prototype

In weeks, not quarters: a functional proof of concept, measured against real data, that confirms or destroys the hypothesis quickly and at low cost.

PHASE 03

Product

What survives the prototype becomes a system: robust, secure, integrated and maintained. The Lab's research always ends in working software.
