The company

Born in the Alentejo. Built for the world.

PingSales AI Solutions, Lda was born in the CIBT NERBE building in Beja - proof that the next generation of Portuguese artificial intelligence companies does not need to be born in a capital city.

We are an artificial intelligence company: we design and train autonomous agents, integrate AI into real operations, develop SaaS platforms, intelligent CRM and ERP systems, research in our lab and train the teams that will work side by side with these systems. Our product brand, PingSales.ai, is the most visible example - AI agents that respond, qualify and sell.

We work with a simple conviction: artificial intelligence is only worth the time it gives back to people. Every project starts by understanding the business - and only then do we train the first model. And we do not work alone: we partner with local agents who want to sell AI solutions in their markets.
